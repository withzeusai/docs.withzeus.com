---
title: 'Build Your First Agent'
---

Follow these simple steps to deploy your first AI automation. 

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login or Sign Up">
    Click [here](https://console.withzeus.com) to login/sign up to Zeus.


    (If you don't have access yet, join the waitlist from our main page [withzeus.com](https://withzeus.com/))
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Agent">
    Click "Create Agent"
    {/* <Screenshot /> */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define what your agent does">
    Describe exactly what you want your agent to accomplish each times it runs

    **Example**
    > *You are a customer support agent. You help customers resolve account issues, answer product questions, track orders, and manage refunds. You are polite and friendly.*

<img src="/images/platform/build-prompt-2-header.png" />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add some tools">
    Click the "tools" tab. Add any tools your agent will need to accomplish its task like Gmail, Zendesk, Salesforce, or Slack. The agent will be able to take action in these apps.

  <img src="/images/platform/old/Build-Tools.png" />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add some knowledge">
    Click the "knowledge" tab. Add any knowledge your agent will need to accomplish its tasks. This can include uploaded files, websites it should be able to read, or pre-written questions and answers that the agent can use.
<img src="/images/platform/old/Build-Knowledge.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview">
    Start playing with your agent using the Preview component on the right. Ask it a question in Chatbot mode or talk to it in  voice mode!
    <Screenshot />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Click the "deploy" tab. Choose what you want to trigger your agent. This can be a set schedule, an API, a 3rd party app (like every time you receive an email), or a phone call to a specific phone number

  <img src="/images/platform/deploy-chatbot-2-header.png" />
  </Step>
</Steps>


That's it! Your agent is live. It can be deployed wherever you want and run whenever you want!
