description: "Learn about the various deployment options available for Zeus AI, including cloud-hosted, on-premises, and hybrid solutions to meet your organization's specific security, compliance, and infrastructure requirements."
---

Zeus AI offers flexible deployment options to accommodate your organization's specific requirements and constraints. Our cloud-hosted solution provides the quickest path to implementation with automatic updates and scalability managed by our team. For organizations with strict data sovereignty or compliance requirements, our on-premises deployment allows you to run Zeus AI within your own infrastructure while maintaining complete control over your data. We also support hybrid deployments that combine the benefits of both approaches, allowing sensitive operations to remain on your infrastructure while leveraging our cloud for other capabilities. Each option includes comprehensive security measures, and our team will work with you to determine the optimal deployment strategy based on your performance needs, compliance requirements, and existing technology stack.
